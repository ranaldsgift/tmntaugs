We can do this the easy way or the hard way Battle 3:1 IDW Games
The Foot Clan has taken Master Splinter, and the turtles have no idea where to look for him. Since the Purple Dragons are a good source of intel, they may know the location of Foot HQ.
Rules
This is a crossover campaign requires both TMNT Adventures: Change is Constant and TMNT Adventures: City Fall to play. This battle must be played with 6 heroes. Splinter may not be played. Since this is Purple Dragons' turf, all villain figures receive Attack/Hit +1. COMPETITIVE MODE: At the start of the battle, the villain player places 1 objective token under each Purple Dragon Gang figure with the question mark side facing up. COOPERATIVE MODE: At the start of the battle, the hero players shuffle the objective tokens and randomly place 1 under each Purple Dragon Gang figure with the question mark side facing up. When a Purple Dragon Gang figure with an objective token is KO'ed, the token is revealed. If the revealed token is red or blue, it is given to the heroes. If the revealed token is green, it is returned to the box.
