1 Select Heroes

Click on the Heroes that will be used in this adventure. Clicking a Hero multiple times will add multiple copies of their initiative card to the initiative deck. Right-clicking on a Hero will remove copies of their initiative card from the deck.

2 Select Villains

Click on the Villains that will be used in this adventure. Clicking a Villain multiple times will add multiple copies of their initiative card to the initiative deck. Right-clicking on a Villain will remove copies of their initiative card from the deck.

3 Select Event Decks

Click on any event decks that you would like to include. The rules recommend using the Base and Hero Decks, and a combination of 3 of the other decks. If you select any event decks here, 2 event triggers will be added to the initiative deck. You can adjust the amount of event triggers by using the controls above the initiative deck.

4 Ready!

Shuffle both decks and click on the initiative deck to draw the top card and start playing! You can click the Hide Setup button below to hide these setup options.
